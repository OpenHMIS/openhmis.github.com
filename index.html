OpenHMIS

An open source hospital management information system based on OpenMRS

View GitHub Profile

The Problem

For the nearly one billion patients in sub-Saharan Africa, bad health care data is not merely a matter of bad service; it is a matter of life and death.

This absence of a widely available and affordable Health Management Information System (HMIS) in Africa poses two significant barriers to improved health care delivery on the continent:

  1. Poor business management software leads to inefficient management of Africaâ€™s health care resources; and
  2. The lack of solid clinical information systems integrated into the daily activities of mainstream health institutions renders sustained improvement of clinical outcomes difficult.

Our Solution

The OpenHMIS project will demonstrate the feasibility of developing and implementing a high-quality, affordable HMIS software package in a low-resource African health care setting that can simultaneously administer the following needs of African healthcare institutions:

  1. Business management
  2. Clinical data management

OpenHMIS will be an open source, fully-integrated HMIS developed by African hospitals for African health institutions.
